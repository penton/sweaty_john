What's it all about

This is the place to talk about your struggle. This idea is purposefully created so you can use it as a tool or starting point for your life changing decision! In simpler terms:

  • Achievable excercises and workouts for any level of fatness so that you can feel the effect of each workout.
  • A personalized diet tailored to your needs and food preferences. (Eating healthy and tasty food is not impossible)
  • No upselling with miracle creams or pills, just hard and enjoyable work on yourself.
  • Psychological help in order to strenghten your will againt overeating junk food. (Life's too short for cutting off certain food, knowing how to control those urges makes us free)

Services we offer

First we have a conversation on Zoom face to face in order to know you. After establishing the base of your problem (i.e. were you always out of shape or other roots of the problem) we'll work on the ideal solution for you. This includes your diet and workouts, it also includes the mental help in order to never fall back again.
